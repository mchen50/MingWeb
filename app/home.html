Where did "CM" come from? What inspired the invention of this LOGO?

"Ming Chen" is actually "Chen Ming". When Ming was back in China, people usually put the last name first and the first name last. Friends of Ming found that interesting to call "CM" (the standard measuring unit), so it became the nickname of Ming. "CM" is a small unit used by people all over the world, which builds up meter, kilometer and the whole mathematical world. Ming uses "CM" to motivate and measure himself.
