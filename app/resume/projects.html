March 2015 - April 2015

Illinois Institute of Technology, Chicago, IL

Summary: A wonderful experience on rushing a complex project in a very limited time.

Technology: php, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, MySQL Workbench, Apache, Git, XAMPP

Details:

  • Designed a database for a restaurant review forum and a laptop marketplace in at least 3NF and put in E/R diagram.

Energy Efficiency of Varied Buildings

January 2014 - May 2014

Illinois Institute of Technology, Chicago, IL

- Massively improve the energy consumption of MTCC (Interprofessional Project)

Summary: Ok, let me tell you how professional lighting retrofit engineers will do to estimate power consumption. They count lights one by one in an actual building. Why not just read plans? Because the electric plans you can obtain are most likely out of date. It will be more accurate to count them by hand.

Technology: CAD, Electric Plan, Photoshop, Building Power Retrofit

Details:

  • Analyzed energy usage and lighting intensity of the lighting system in McCormick Tribune Campus Center.
  • Put forward a feasible proposal included cost-benefit analysis and lighting plan design.
  • Consulted Electrical Contractors Association (ECA) and Connecxion for advices to NECA Green Energy Challenge.

Thoughts: Our team worked on lighting retrofit. We consulted professional lighting retrofit company - Connecxion. They came to IIT and helped us about it. I also worked really close with an electric technician who had 30 years of on field experience. He was one of my teammates. I still clearly remember what he told me when I complained about all other team members were not doing work: "In the real life, no matter how the work gets done, it just needs to be done".
